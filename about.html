ABOUT ME

Welcome to my world of photography and visual art!
I am Senna, a passionate artist who embarked on
my creative journey in Kazakhstan and now
continues to explore and capture the beauty of
different landscapes across Europe. Whether it's
the vibrant streets of Klaipeda or the breathtaking
scenery of other European destinations, I find
inspiration everywhere I go.Balancing my love
for photography with my studies at LCC
International University, I constantly strive
to learn and grow as an artist. Through my lens,
I aim to share my unique perspective, capturing
the essence of each moment and creating visual
stories that resonate with viewers.
Join me on this creative adventure as we embark
on a visual voyage through time and space.
